Tacos

several delicious tacos

These are real easy to make, and dont take much time for such a fantastic result

Ingredients

  • hamburger
  • shells (hard or soft)
  • cheese
  • veggies of your choosing
  • sauce

Steps

  1. brown meat
  2. add seasoning/water

  4. prep veggies/cheese
  5. ENJOY
