Sandwich

one of many options of Sandwich

These are real easy to make, and dont take much time for such a fantastic result

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • butter/mayo
  • cheese
  • veggies of your choosing
  • meat(s)

Steps

  1. get 2 slices of bread
  2. butter or add mayo
  3. add meat
  4. add cheese
  5. add veggies
  6. ENJOY
